## Step-by-Step Guide (15 Minutes)

### Step 1: Sign Up & Choose Template (2 minutes)

1. **Go to**: https://www.canva.com/
2. **Sign up**: Free account
3. **Search**: "Startup Pitch Deck" or "Investor Pitch Deck"
4. **Choose**: Modern template with purple/blue colors
5. **Click**: "Customize this template"

**Recommended Templates**:
- "Modern Startup Pitch Deck"
- "Tech Startup Presentation"
- "Investor Pitch Deck Purple"

---

### Step 2: Customize Brand Colors (1 minute)

1. Click on any colored element
2. Click color picker
3. Enter TourChain colors:
   - Primary: `#6366f1` (Purple)
   - Secondary: `#f59e0b` (Orange)
   - Accent: `#10b981` (Green)
4. Click "Add to brand kit"
5. Apply to all slides

---

### Step 3: Add TourChain Logo (1 minute)

1. **Get Logo**:
   - Go to http://localhost:3000
   - Right-click logo → Save image
   - Or use: `apps/web/public/logo.png`

2. **Upload to Canva**:
   - Click "Uploads" in left sidebar
   - Click "Upload files"
   - Select logo file
   - Drag onto slide

3. **Position**:
   - Top-left corner
   - Resize to ~150px wide
   - Copy to all slides (Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V)

---

### Step 4: Edit Slide Content (10 minutes)

## SLIDE 1: Title Slide

**Replace**:
- Company name → "TOURCHAIN"
- Tagline → "Blockchain-Powered Tourism for Nepal"
- Subtitle → "Trustless trekking payments on Solana"

**Add**:

## SLIDE 2: Problem

**Replace**:
- Title → "THE PROBLEM"
- Subtitle → "Nepal's Tourism Industry Faces Critical Trust Issues"

**Add 4 Problem Cards**:
1. Click "Elements" → Search "card"
2. Add 4 cards in 2x2 grid
3. Add icons: 💰 🚫 📄 🏆
4. Add text:
   - Payment Disputes (40%)
   - Fake Guides (25% increase)
   - Manual Verification (3-5 days)
   - No Proof of Achievement

**Add Chart**:
- Click "Elements" → Search "chart"
- Choose bar chart or line graph
- Edit data to show growth

### Step 5: Add Animations (1 minute)

1. Click any element
2. Click "Animate" in top toolbar
3. Choose animation:
   - Entrance: "Fade" or "Rise"
   - Duration: 0.5-1 second
4. Apply to key elements

---

### Step 6: Export (1 minute)

**Option 1: PowerPoint**
1. Click "Share" → "Download"
2. Choose "Microsoft PowerPoint (.pptx)"
3. Click "Download"

**Option 2: PDF**
1. Click "Share" → "Download"
2. Choose "PDF Standard"
3. Click "Download"

**Option 3: Present Online**
1. Click "Present"
2. Choose "Present and record"
3. Share link with audience

## Pro Tips

### Design Tips:
- **Consistency**: Use same fonts throughout
- **White Space**: Don't overcrowd slides
- **Hierarchy**: Make important text larger
- **Alignment**: Keep elements aligned
- **Colors**: Stick to 3 main colors

### Content Tips:
- **One Idea Per Slide**: Don't overload
- **Big Numbers**: Make metrics stand out
- **Visuals**: Use images over text
- **Stories**: Tell compelling narratives
- **Call to Action**: Clear next steps

### Presentation Tips:
- **Practice**: Rehearse 3-5 times
- **Timing**: 1-2 minutes per slide
- **Backup**: Download PDF backup
- **Demo**: Have live demo ready
- **Questions**: Prepare FAQ answers

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Can't find good template
**Solution**: Search "tech startup pitch deck" or "blockchain presentation"

**Problem**: Images look pixelated
**Solution**: Use high-resolution images (1920x1080 minimum)

**Problem**: Too much text on slide
**Solution**: Use bullet points, remove unnecessary words

**Problem**: Colors don't match
**Solution**: Use color picker to match exact hex codes

**Problem**: Can't export as PowerPoint (free account)
**Solution**: Export as PDF, or upgrade to Canva Pro ($12.99/month)
