# Quick Screenshot Checklist for TourChain Presentation

## 🚀 Take These 10 Screenshots (15 Minutes)

### Setup:
1. Open http://localhost:3000 in Chrome
2. Press F11 for fullscreen
3. Hide bookmarks bar (Ctrl+Shift+B)
4. Use Windows + Shift + S to capture

## Can't Take Screenshots?

### Alternative 1: Use Placeholder Images
- Go to Canva
- Search "app mockup" or "dashboard mockup"
- Use generic tech images temporarily
- Replace later with real screenshots

### Alternative 2: Screen Recording
1. Press Windows + G (Game Bar)
2. Click record
3. Navigate through app
4. Extract frames from video

### Alternative 3: Ask Team Member
- Share screen via Zoom/Meet
- Have them take screenshots
- Send you the images

---

## Screenshot Quality Tips:

✅ **DO**:
- Use 1920x1080 resolution
- Full screen (F11)
- Clean browser (no extensions visible)
- Good lighting on screen

❌ **DON'T**:
- Include personal data
- Show browser bookmarks
- Capture with notifications
- Use low resolution
